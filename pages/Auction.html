Auto Auction Houses Requirements to Release Vehicles

What are the requirements from the auction house for releasing the vehicle from the auction:

While picking up a vehicle from the Auto auction, the carrier must have three important info’s, lot number, buyer number, and release form. These are presented at the time of pickup of the vehicle from the auto auction.

The car shipping company must provide the auto auction with specific information to release the vehicle for shipping.

1. Lot number

The first and most important information to release the vehicle from the auto auction is the lot number, which specifies the vehicle's physical location in the lot. Auto carriers need the lot number to inform the auction house to release the vehicle they are required to pick up.

2. Buyer Number

The Recommended way to Ship your Car from Auto Auction

Purchasing a car from an auto auction is a continuous process throughout the year. If you are searching to ship your car safely at a good price from any auto auction in the US, then you have to understand the different shipping methods and their pros and cons. You also need to know how to choose a reliable auto shipper. In an auction, when the vehicle is purchased, the buyer must pick up the vehicle within five working days; if he fails to pick it up within five working days after purchase, the vehicle is shifted to the warehouse, where every day after the fifth day the amount of $25 is charged from the buyer. So, it is necessary to understand the best way to save any additional charges. First, you must determine if your vehicle is a luxury, exotic antique, or costly car, then choose enclosed car transport options; otherwise, you must go with open auto transport, the commonly used auto shipping method. Secondly, you should choose expedited auto shipping, in which the vehicle is picked up urgent basis. That’s the best to save additional charges without any delays in pickup.
