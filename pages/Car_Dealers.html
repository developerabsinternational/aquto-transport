The Different Methods of Transporting Cars for Dealerships

Car dealerships use different methods to carry out the car transport process.

Open and Enclosed Shipping

The car dealers used open and enclosed shipping methods. The choice between open and enclosed shipping depends on the vehicle's size, weight, and value. Enclosed carriers transport luxury and classical cars. The cost of shipping a vehicle with open shipping is lower than enclosed shipping. On the other hand, enclosed car shipping provides more safety than open carriers.

Boat Shipping

Most dealers used the boat shipping option. This method is the fastest and allows for the simultaneous shipping of multiple cars. The vehicles are transported on a roll-on/roll-off vessel, which is cost-effective for transporting bulk vehicle shipments.

Rail Transportation

Another method of transporting your vehicle is by car. This method transports the car in a container and by train. It is the most convenient and easiest way of shipping a vehicle.
