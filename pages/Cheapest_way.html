Cheapest Way to Ship a Car

Terminal to Terminal Car Shipping

If you need to transport your car to a new location, you should know the cheapest way to ship a car. Look no further than terminal-to-terminal car shipping and open auto transport options! In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits of this affordable car shipping option and show you how to prepare your car for a smooth and hassle-free shipping experience.

What is Terminal to Terminal Car Shipping?

Terminal-to-terminal car shipping is a type of car shipping service where your vehicle is picked up from a designated terminal and delivered to another terminal at the destination. The terminals are usually located in major cities or transportation hubs, such as ports or airports. Nowadays, this is the cheapest way to ship a car in the USA.

Best and most Economical option to ship a car

One of the main reasons terminal-to-terminal car shipping is cheaper than other types of car shipping services is because it eliminates the need for door-to-door service. With door-to-door service, the carrier has to pick up your vehicle from your home or office and deliver it to your desired location. This requires more time, effort, and resources, which makes it more expensive.

On the other hand, with terminal-to-terminal car shipping, you have to drop off your vehicle at the designated terminal and pick it up from the destination terminal. This reduces the carrier's workload and expenses, which results in lower shipping costs That is why it's the cheapest way to ship a car

What are the Benefits of Terminal to Terminal Car Shipping

Apart from being cheaper, terminal-to-terminal car shipping has several other benefits, such as:

1. Flexibility

Terminal-to-terminal car shipping gives you more flexibility regarding pick-up and delivery dates and times. You can drop off your vehicle at the terminal at your convenience and pick it up from the destination terminal when it suits you.

2. Security

Terminals are usually secure facilities with cameras, security guards, and other safety measures in place. This ensures that your vehicle is safe during transit.

3. Reliability

Terminal-to-terminal car shipping is a reliable service, as carriers have a fixed schedule and route for pick-up and delivery. This reduces the chances of delays or cancellations.

4. Cost-effective

As mentioned earlier, terminal-to-terminal car shipping is an affordable and cheapest way to ship a car, especially if you are on a tight budget.

How to Prepare Your Vehicle for Terminal-to-Terminal Car Shipping?

Clean your car

Ensure it is clean inside and out, as the carrier may inspect it before shipping. Remove any personal belongings from the car, as insurance does not cover them.

Check for damages

Inspect your car for any damage or scratches and take photos. This will help you file a claim if any new damages occur during transit.

Check your fue level

Make sure your fuel level is no more than a quarter tank, as carriers have to transport your vehicle with a lower fuel level for safety reasons

Remove accessories

Remove non-permanent accessories from your car, such as bike racks, spoilers, or antennas. This will prevent them from getting damaged during transit.

Disable alarms

 If your car has an alarm system, disable it before dropping it off at the terminal.
