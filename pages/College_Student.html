How Much Does It Cost?

There is a common misconception about the service charges of auto transport. People think that such services cost a lot as compared to driving the vehicle oneself. However, the total cost of driving the car to its distant location will be, more or less, the same as compared to the shipping quote, and your extra efforts on the long drive will pay up nothing but deep tiredness and maintenance expenses of the vehicle.

Auto shipping companies charge around one dollar per mile for the first 500 miles, and after that, the per unit rate of miles falls as the distance increases. However, distance is not the sole determiner of the quote, as several other factors can slightly or considerably change the rate. Such factors are the make and model of the car, its running condition, its size and weight, and its mode of transportation.

What to Keep in Mind Before Shipping?

As mentioned before, car shipping is a reliable but complex process.

  • The college student should decide where he wants the delivery and finalize the deal accordingly.
  • Mostly, auto shipping is done from terminal to terminal, so the student has to visit the terminal to receive the car, but in door-to-door service, the shipping company drops off the vehicles at the college campus or rented house.
  • The auto transporter takes 2 to 7 days for the transit, so the student should plan his journey according to the time frame of the car's delivery.
  • Auto shipping companies don't have access to all the sites, so in case of door-to-door shipping, some nearby point has to be chosen.

In the end, the experience of using an auto shipping service can either be good or bad, depending upon the hauler one chooses.
