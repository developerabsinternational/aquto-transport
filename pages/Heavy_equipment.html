How long does it take to transport Heavy Equipment

The time to transport heavy equipment from one location to another one mainly depends on distance. After that other factors also influence the shipping duration.

Distance from pickup to delivery point: This is a major factor that is main contributor in time of transportation.

How long does it take to Ship Heavy Equipment across the country?

The estimated time to ship Heavy Equipment across the country differs on the basis of various factors. However, the major factor is the distance from the pickup origin to delivery point. Other elements including the winter season, type of shipping service, and route planning, these factors also have impact on the time to ship an oversized bulky equipment from one point to other one.

  • It will take 1 day to 3 days for a short distance (The distance between two conjoin states).
  • A medium distance is considered the distance more than 100 miles but less than 1000 mile. When distance is medium It will take 3 days to 7 days for a medium distance.
  • It might take 7- 16 working days of time from pick up to delivery from east coast to west coast (2430 miles of distance) and 9-18 days of time period from north to south. The distance more than 1000 miles is categorized as long distance.

Type of Transport Service

The type of shipping service also impacts shipping time duration; whether it is expedited or standard shipping. In expedited shipping, the pickup-to-delivery duration is less as compared to standard shipping. The expedited transport is done on an urgent basis and incurs high shipping costs in comparison to standard transportation. The recommended option to move a tractor or any other equipment is standard shipping, if you are not in a hurry to move your Equipment

1. Winter Season

If you are planning to transport in winter, the swear weather condition, heavy snowfall or storm can cause delay in overall shipping time of your shipment.

2. Route and Permits

The route chosen for transportation also has a vital role in the overall duration of transportation. If the chosen route has high traffic, it will increase the total time of transportation.
