How to Ship A CAR

We simplify all the procedures and steps to ship a car in one click. We use a detailed and fluent approach to describe the whole shipping process, comprised of a few simple steps.

Step 1

Find a reliable Auto Shipping Company Online

The first step in the shipping process is to find a reliable auto transport company. Find and click on their official site link to visit their website.

Get an Instant Quote

Step 2

Book an Order

The second step in the auto-shipping process is to book an order online. Several customers get confused about how to fill out the booking form to complete the order correctly. If you put the wrong information while submitting the order, it can result in high costs and delays in shipping. Almost all transport companies require the same type of information to book an order for car shipping.

Here, we thoroughly explain every step of order booking to ease customers’ worries and avoid them during the booking process.

1

It is the first step in proceed to order booking. Here, a graphic representation shows the option to fill out the information of pickup and delivery addresses, which comprises zip code, state, and city. Suppose you want to ship a car from the City of Controlee, Maryland, to Boffola City, California. You will select the state first and then enter the zip code for pickup and delivery.

2

The second step in filling out the quote form is to submit the information of the vehicle's make, model, and year of manufacture. Submit it carefully because vehicles with different models have different sizes. In case of wrong information submission, it can delay the shipping process because the carrier makes a proper shipping plan and allocates specific space for every vehicle as per its size requirement.

3

In the third step, the option of “carrier type” will appear. In the quote image, you can see the open and enclosed options. So, choose the right carrier option according to your requirements and budget. If you want a detailed understanding of both carrier types, read more to explore them briefly Open auto transport Enclosed Auto Transport

4

The fourth and final step is related to payment. The image shows all the required information for this step. When you complete this step, your order will be confirmed and called an active order.

Step 3

Schedule Pickup

When you confirm your booking with an auto transport company, they will schedule the pickup for your vehicle according to the date your mentioned date of pickup. If you want to change or customize the pickup or delivery address after the confirmation of the order, you can call, email, or message the auto-transport team. After the pickup schedule, the transport company assigns a driver to pick up your vehicle and also provides you with a driver's insurance certificate. In case of any damage during the shipping from pickup to delivery, you can claim insurance for that loss.

Step 4

Vehicle Pickup

The fourth and final step is related to payment. The image shows all the required information for this step. When you complete this step, your order will be confirmed and called an active order.

Step 5

Delivery of Vehicle

Vehicle delivery is the final step in the car shipping process. In this final step, the driver delivers the vehicle at the mentioned destination and asks the to sign the bill of lading in which he lists information at the time of vehicle pick-up. First, inspect your vehicle briefly. If you find the vehicle is in the same condition as it was at the time of pickup, then sign the BOL; otherwise, contact the carrier company quickly.
