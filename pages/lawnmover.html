Precautions for a Carrier

Legal Considerations

The carrier must fully comply with the DOT weight and working hours limit law for shipping equipment; otherwise, it can result in a fine.

Safety Approach

Carriers must follow all safety standards and approaches to enhance shipping safety for the secure shipment of equipment.

Visibility

Ensure the mower is not hanging out and doesn't cause obstacles to the driver's front view.

Secure the Lawn Mower

Secure the mower with straps soundly so it cannot move during transit.

Wheel Chocks

Chock the wheel with a wheel chock so it does not move during shipping.

Load and Unload

To avoid damage during loading and unloading, use the loading ramps for riding or heavy mowers.

Cost to ship a lawn mower across the country

The number of these factors measures the transportation cost of lawn mower shipping; distance of your shipment from the origin of pickup to the destination of delivery. Every inuring mile will add cost of transportation. The size and weight of a lawn mower also impact the total shipping cost; if it's a small mower, the cost will be low, but the cost will be high for a heavy one like a commercial mower. The shipping method, locations, and method of equipment transport, whether it will be shipped by road or another approach, will be used for its shipping. The most recommended and cheapest way is by road transport. If a mower requires additional packing, it increases shipping costs.
