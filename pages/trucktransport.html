Regulation to Transport Trucks in America

Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration Working hours limits for drivers: FMCSA limits drivers' working hours to prevent them from being fatigued to increase transport safety, otherwise it can result boost in road accidents and damages. Electronic logging devices are installed in trucks that track driver working hours. If any driver is found to work exceeding the allowed working hours per day, he will be penalized and his driving license will be cancelled.
